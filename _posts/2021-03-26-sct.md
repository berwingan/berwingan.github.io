In my senior spring semester, I took a directed study class with Professor Lefteris Kirousis in the subject of social choice theory. We worked mostly on voting theory and judgement theory. For each class, based on the previous week's readings, I would prepare slides to showcase what I understand from the readings and present them to Professor Lefteris. Bellow are these slides which for most of them have errs in them. 

Some of my slides are based on Professor Lefteris' paper on [aggregation with multiple positions.](/assets/pdf/aggregation.pdf)
